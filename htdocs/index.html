About

Crowder is a community based video streaming platform. Every user that views videos is not just downloading the parts he needs to view the video, but is also uploading those parts that he already downloaded to other users needing them. The technology behind it is based on the bittorrent technology and is in fact compatible with it.

This setup allows for a very robust network, because videos that are currently interesting and get watched a lot, are also being shared alot. On top of that, popular videos are also loading faster, because there is a higher chance that someone near you is sharing his parts of the videos directly with you, which is a lot faster than connecting to one central distant server.

Legal questions

Bittorrent is often falsely associated with illegal sharing of copyrighted material. It is important to note though, that the protocol itself is completely legal and is not posing any threat to you as a user. On this website, we are making sure that there is no copyrighted material is uploaded without the consent of the copyright-holder.
What this means for you as a user is, that you will not find many music-videos or TV-shows here, since they all fall into the copyrighted category. We are also against geoblocking and as such are not hosting videos that have licenses that would restrict playback to certain countries.
